About Us

KABRA & PATNI is a Chartered Accountant firm registered with Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). The firm established in the year 1999, have head office at AURANGABAD and Branches at SANGLI & WASHIM. The firm provides a wide range of services like Audit and Assurance, Direct and Indirect Taxation, Project Financing, Company Law Matters, Information Systems Consultancy, Accounting and Related Consultancy, and Business Process Outsourcing. The service model is flexible and allows for periodic change and rebalancing. In addition, the firm has transformed itself into an integrated enterprise which has improved overall productivity. As a result, today the firm is a high performing enterprise.

WE provide all types of Assurance Services, Internal & Statutory Audits, Information Systems & Security Audits, Business Advisory Services, Business Consultancy, Project Evaluation & Feasibility Studies, Direct & Indirect Taxation. We have a solid background in supplying a complete service support related to the opening of a new business in the form of Proprietorships, Partnership firms & Pvt. Ltd. companies.
